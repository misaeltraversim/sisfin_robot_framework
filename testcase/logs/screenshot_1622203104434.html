 PASSWORD                                                                       
                                                                                
        If you have been provided a password, you will be required to enter     
        it when you logon to TSO.  Your password will not be visible on         
        your terminal during your logon.                                        
                                                                                
        If your userid is defined to RACF (Resource Access Control              
        Facility), your installation may have defined password rules            
        specifying the minimum/maximum password length and/or the               
        acceptable characters.  In addition, if the RACF mixed case             
        password option is enabled, lower case alphabetic characters will       
        not be translated to upper case.                                        
                                                                                
        If you have been provided a password phrase, it could be up to 100      
        characters long and can include punctuation or special characters.      
                                                                                
        If your userid is not defined to RACF, your password is 1 to 8          
        alphanumeric or national characters (A-Z, 0-9 , #, $, @).  Any          
        combination of these characters can be used in the password.
